Welcome. This guided funeral policy application usually takes about 10 to 15 minutes and will require details of everyone who needs to be covered.

Before You Begin

Have these details ready:
  • Main member first name and surname
  • South African ID number or passport details
  • Residential and contact information
  • Dependant and extended family details where applicable
  • Beneficiary and payment information for the final steps
What happens next:
  • You will complete the member, family, beneficiary, and payment details step by step.
  • You will verify the application with a one-time pin before submission.
  • Your policy documents will be generated after the final confirmation step.
Privacy Notice: Your information is handled under South African data protection requirements and is used only for policy onboarding and servicing.
